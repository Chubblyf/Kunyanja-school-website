Welcome to Kunyanja Pvt School

Kunyanja Pvt School offers a holistic education from primary to secondary level in beautiful Northern Malawi. Here's what you need to know to apply:

  1. Eligibility: We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth.
  2. Application Process: To apply, please complete the online application form or obtain a physical copy from our school office. Ensure all required documents, such as academic transcripts, birth certificates, and recommendation letters, are submitted along with the application.
  3. Admission Criteria: Admission is based on various factors, including academic performance, character assessment, and potential for contribution to the school community. We prioritize students who demonstrate a passion for learning and embody our school values.
  4. Entrance Examinations: Applicants may be required to sit for entrance examinations to assess their academic abilities. These tests help us determine the appropriate placement within our curriculum.
  5. Interviews: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for interviews with our admissions committee. This is an opportunity for us to learn more about each student and their aspirations.
  6. Fees and Scholarships: Details regarding tuition fees, payment options, and available scholarships can be found on our website or obtained from the admissions office. We strive to make quality education accessible to deserving students through our scholarship programs.
  7. Important Dates: Stay updated on application deadlines, examination dates, and admission notifications by checking our website regularly or contacting our admissions team.
